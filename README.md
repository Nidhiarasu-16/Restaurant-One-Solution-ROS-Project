# ROS Business Analytics Pipeline and Dashboard

## Project Overview :

The ROS Business Analytics Pipeline is an end-to-end data engineering, analytics, and business intelligence project developed to simulate and analyze operational data generated by the ROS (Restaurant Operating System) platform.

The project covers the complete analytics lifecycle, including data generation, database design, anomaly detection, data transformation, workflow automation using KNIME, workflow testing, Power BI data modeling, and interactive dashboard development.

The solution demonstrates how raw transactional business data can be transformed into actionable insights through a structured ETL and analytics pipeline.

---

## Business Context :

ROS provides ERP and operational management solutions for restaurants. The platform manages restaurant operations including:

* Client Management
* Restaurant Management
* User Management
* Subscription Management
* Orders and Sales Tracking
* Cash-Up Processes
* Banking Reconciliation
* Delivery Management
* Expense Tracking
* Tax Management

The objective of this project is to build a scalable analytics solution capable of transforming operational data into meaningful business intelligence.
